Master Planning

KCKV Consultants is a leading architectural design consultancy firm in Delhi providing comprehensive professional services for the last 28 years. KCKV Consultants is a team of experienced Architects, Urban Designers, Interior Designers, providing design consultancy services in India and all around the world.

KCKV Consultants is a leading architecture and Urban Design firm. The firm has been providing consultancy in master planning of townships, SEZ’s and industrial parks. The firm has provided master planning services to many reputed developers. Some of our esteemed clients are Ansals, Omaxe, Parsvnath, TDI, Ansal housing, Terra Infrastructure, Narsi group etc. Our firm has the privilege of designing and executing many townships for them in different states like Haryana, Rajasthan, Punjab, chhattisgarh etc.

The firm offers complete Urban Design, Town planning consultancy and project coordination. It includes analysis of site and requirements, concept design and detail working drawings. Moreover, design of services in terms of structure, public health, electrical networking, transport planning etc. Most importantly, the firm assists the clients from the concept stage to the completion of the project.

Large township projects have been designed which are not only environmentally sensitive and socially responsive but also have efficient infrastructure. The townships have been designed based on client’s brief and also state norms and bye laws. In addition to this the principles of town planning have been applied and executed.

Meanwhile, the design has strong hierarcal road network. In other words it consists of main arterials, sub-arterials and feeder roads. Most importantly the revenue rastas have been integrated in the main design. Similarly the townships have strategic and equal distribution of commercial spaces and facilities. There is hierarchy and equal distribution of greens so that all families can access it. Above all the placement and orientation of plots is as per climitic conditions. To clarify bigger plots are strategically placed on green facing and wider roads. The entry gate of the township has been designed to create a sense of arrival and enhance project aesthetics.

Sample Projects

RESIDENTIAL GATED COMMUNITY

The layout was planned around central green. The 4 different categories of the villa’s were designed in the same modern architectural vocabulary so that the complex looked organized and unified.

PROPOSAL – MALL AT BHONDSI

Modern mall with a glass elliptical two storied sphere to house the anchor shop situated on the main road at the entrance of the Bio-tech SEZ forms a strong landmark and focal point.

GROUP HOUSING AT JAIPUR

The building blocks were placed in an ascending order creating staggered terraces facing the public green. The upper floor with terraces were planned as penthouses.

PROPOSAL- GROUP HOUSING AT NOIDA, U.P.

PROPOSAL - COMMUNITY CENTRE PROJECT

A dynamic structural form was proposed to reflect the modern cultural and social activities being provided in the community center.
